Two non-negotiables

  • Daily 5-min mobility: doorway chest stretch, hip-flexor lunge stretch, chin tucks. Tight chest & hips are half the posture problem.
  • Fix your desk: monitor at eye level, stand up every 30–45 min. You can't out-train 10 hours of slouching.
